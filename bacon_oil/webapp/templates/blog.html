The benefits of fat

More and more research is starting to point to the health benefits of including fat in your diet. Back in the late 1900's there was a big push by the food industry to label fat as the reason why people were getting fatter. As the government mandated that fat be reduced in food, food companies had to find different ways to get that same flavor back into the food. Sugar was their response and over the last 25 years sugar, and other processed forms of sugar was the dominant ingredient in food. If you look at almost any food label today you will see that sugar is on the label. Sugar has an interesting effect on the human body.

Although fat was getting removed from food, people continued to get bigger and bigger. Research has proven that sugar increases the apatite of the consumer rather than satisfying it. After consuming high sugar foods, people continue to keep eating. Fat on the other hand satisfies our craving for food, which can lead to people feeling full. This leads to people consuming less food. Despite what we have been hearing for our whole lives, fat is not the evil food we once thought it was. I recommend doing some more research and let the facts speak for themselves.
