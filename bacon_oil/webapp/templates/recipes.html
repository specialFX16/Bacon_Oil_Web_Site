French Fries with Bacon Oil

Incredients


Bacon oil
French fries or cut potatoes
Seasoning


Directions

STEP 1. Set your cooking pot on the stove over medium heat. Spoon in your bacon oil. Allow to melt like butter

STEP 2. Insert your cooking thermometer. Heat the bacon oil at medium until it reaches 300 degrees F. Adjust the heat to keep it stable at that temperature

STEP 3. Sink a handful of fried into the oil. Cover the top of the pot with your splash screen to protect yourself and the kitchen

STEP 4. Wait until the fries are golden brown -- about 2 to 3 mintues. Remove using your tongs or spider

STEP 5. Set the finished fries on your papertowl to soak up the extra grease. Sprinkle on the seasoning of your choice

STEP 6. Add more fried to your oil, cover with a splash screen, fry until gold brown, drain on papertowll, and season. Continue doing this until you've cooked all your fries. You can strain the bacon oil through a coffee filter if it starts to get dirty

Bacon Oil Injection Recipe

Incredients


2 sticks of butter
1 and 1/2 cups of John Gordon's Bacon Oil
1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
1-2 teaspoons of hot sauce to taste
2 tablespoons of salt
1 tablespoon of black pepper
1/2 teaspoon of red pepper
1/2 teaspoon of white pepper


Directions

STEP 1. With the completely thawed turkey, inject liquid mixture into turkey making sure to get needle into the meat of the turkey. Inject breast, thighs, legs and wings until all liquid mixture is used.

STEP 2. Heat oil to 375 degreese. DO NOT HEAT ABOVE 400 DEGREES. Maintain oil temperature at 350 degreese during frying process. Deep fry turkey for 2 and 3/4 minutes for each pound of turkey weight.
