Safety Tips

- Keep oil temperature below 380 degrees. If temperature is above 380, turn off heat and allow the temperature to come back down.

- Never use ice or any form of water to try and cool the oil down.

- Make sure burner is on a flat hard service away from any structures

- When frying, turn off the burner and slowly lower the food into the oil. When fully submerged, carefully turn the burner back on.

- When cooking a turkey try to keep size of the turkey to around 10-12 lbs. For each pound of turkey cook approximately 3 minutes per pound and Keep temperatures at 350 degrees Fahrenheit.

- DO NOT leave unattended when heating or frying

- DO NOT refill original container with hot oil

- IF OIL SMOKES : Reduce Heat

- IF OIL CATCHES FIRE: Turn off Heat, Cover until cool. Only use fire extinguisher that are designated for use on oil. NEVER spray water on burning or hot oil
