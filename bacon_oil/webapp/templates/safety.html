Safety Tips

DO NOT over fill ot with oil - lowering the turkey will displace oil.

DO NOT over heat. If oil begins to smoke, turn off flame.

Use fireproof gloves and eye protection when moving turkey in and out of pot. Turn off the flame when moving the turkey in and out of the pot.

Never use propane indoors.

Never leave fryer unattended.

Keep children away from hot oil at all times.

DO NOT put water on hot or burning flame

Oil wil remain hot for period of time even without flame. Take caution at allt imes with hot oil.
