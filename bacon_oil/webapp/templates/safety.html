Safety Tips

- Keep oil temperature below 380 degrees. If temperature is above 380, turn off heat and allow the temperature to come back down.

- Never use ice or any form of water to try and cool the oil down.

- Make sure burner is on a flat hard service away from any structures

- When frying, turn off the burner and slowly lower turkey into the oil. When fully submerged, carefully turn the burner back on

- When cooking a turkey try to keep size of the turkey to around 10-12 lbs. Cook for 2 minutes and 45 seconds per pound. Keep temperature between 325 - 350
